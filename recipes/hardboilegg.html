Hard Boil Egg

Steps:

  1. Place the egg in a pot and add water until it is 1 inch above the egg.
  2. Bring the pot to a boil at medium high heat and remove the pot from the heat.
  3. Cover the pot with a lid and cook the egg in the pot for 14 minutes.
  4. Transfer the egg to an ice bath for 15 minutes.
  5. Peel the eggshell off before serving.
