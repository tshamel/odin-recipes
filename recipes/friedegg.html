Sunny Side Up Egg

Steps:

  1. Melt 1 teaspoon of butter in a frying pan at medium low heat.
  2. Crack the egg into the frying pan.
  3. Spoon the melted butter over the top of the egg while the bottom cooks.
  4. Cook until the egg whites are firm but the egg yolk is runny.
  5. Season the egg with salt and pepper before serving.
