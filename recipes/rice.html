Rice

Steps:

  1. In a rice cooker, rinse the rice in cold water.
  2. Pour away the rinsing water from the rice cooker.
  3. Add water to the rice cooker (rice:water 1:1).
  4. Set the rice cooker to the rice cooking option and press start.
  5. When the rice is ready, use a rice paddle to fluff the rice (scoop the rice from bottom to the top) before serving.
