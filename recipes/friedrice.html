Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

The second best fried rice after good old egg fried rice!

Ingredients (1 serving):

  • 1/2 cup of uncooked rice
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup kimchi (at least 2 weeks old)
  • 1/4 cup kimchi juice
  • 2 shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 chicken fillet
  • 2 teaspoons gochujang
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 1 pinch MSG
  • 1 egg
  • 1 scallion
  • 2 seaweed

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice 1 day before and keep refrigerated until cooking.
  2. Add the sesame oil to the rice and mix it in (break up the rice and ensure they do not clump up).
  3. Squeeze the kimchi juice out from the kimchi and place it in a separate bowl.
  4. Cut the kimchi, mushrooms and chicken fillet into small pieces.
  5. Mix the kimchi juice, gochujang and soy sauce to make kimchi sauce.
  6. Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan at medium high heat.
  7. Add the kimchi and mushrooms to the frying pan and stirfry well.
  8. Add the chicken fillet to the frying pan once the kimchi and mushrooms start to caramelise and stirfry until chicken fillet is cooked.
  9. Add the rice to the frying pan and toss the rice (shake, forward thrust and upward wrist flick) until it is heated throughly.
  10. Add the kimchi sauce to the frying pan and continue to toss the rice at high heat until all the liquid has evaporated and the ingredients are mixed well.
  11. Season the rice with salt, pepper and MSG and mix well.
  12. Cook the sunny side up egg and place on top of the fried rice.
  13. Cut the scallion and seaweed into small pieces to garnish the fried rice before serving.
