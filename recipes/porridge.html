Chicken and Mushroom Porridge

What is this?

Porridge so good you will want to eat it even when you are not sick!

Ingredients (1 serving):

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup uncooked rice
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 shiitake mushrooms
  • 1 chicken fillet
  • 1 old ginger
  • 1 chicken bone
  • 2 dried red dates
  • 1 dried honey date
  • 2/3 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 1/3 tablespoons black sesame oil
  • 1/3 teaspoon five spice powder

Steps:

  1. Rinse then soak the peanuts in cold water at least 6 hours before cooking.
  2. In a rice cooker, rinse then soak the rice in cold water for 15 minutes.
  3. Cut the carrots, mushrooms and chicken fillet into small pieces.
  4. Peel off the skin of the ginger and cut a piece that is half a thumb size.
  5. Pour away the rinsing water from the rice cooker.
  6. Add water to the rice cooker (rice:water 1:5 or 1:6 if you prefer it to be more soupy).
  7. Add the carrots, mushrooms, ginger, chicken fillet, chicken bone, red dates and honey date to the rice cooker.
  8. Add the soy sauce, sesame oil and five spice powder to the rice cooker.
  9. Stir the contents of the rice cooker well.
  10. Set the rice cooker to the porridge cooking option for 2 hours and press start.
  11. After 2 hours, stir the contents of the rice cooker again before serving.
